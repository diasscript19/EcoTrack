Plant a Tree

EcoTrack gives everyone the opportunity to participate in ecological restoration. The funds raised are used to organize volunteer planting events in different regions of Azerbaijan by a team of activists with whom we collaborate. Trees are planted en masse in areas where there is an urgent need for them. Service users receive certificates confirming their contribution to eco-restoration. After planting, they receive an email report describing the campaign, with photos, a video, and the GPS coordinates of the restored area.
